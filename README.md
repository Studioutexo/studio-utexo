# Studio Utexo

Studio Utexo is a UK-based digital studio founded by Steven Hampson.

We help local service businesses like gyms, restaurants, and trades improve their online presence and convert more website visitors into paying customers.

---

## What we focus on

We specialise in improving:

- Website UX (user experience)
- Conversion rates (turning visitors into leads)
- Lead generation systems
- Online presence for local businesses

---

## The problem we solve

Most small businesses lose customers because of:

- Confusing or outdated websites
- Weak messaging
- No clear call-to-action
- Poor mobile experience
- No follow-up systems for enquiries

We fix this by simplifying and optimising their digital presence.

---

## Our approach

1. Analyse the current website and online presence  
2. Identify conversion problems and friction points  
3. Design clear improvements and fixes  
4. Improve enquiry flow and customer acquisition
